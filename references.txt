# text_tools_project
## Topic

**Tagging Euphemistic Phrases' Part of Speech**

Whitney Poh
Hasan Can Biyik

We will clean up some euphemism data from the NLP Lab (Dr. Anna Feldman, Dr. Jing Peng, et al) using commands like sed and tr, and use a part-of-speech tagger to identify each euphemistic phrase's part of speech.
